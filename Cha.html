Dragon Born


Humanoid Dragons that have natural armor due to their scales. Not only that, they have claws and can breath fire or another element.


Humans


A medium sized creature that starts adulthood at 15 years and lives to 70+2d20 72 to 110 years old. Humans are the generic character due to the fact that it is based on real life.


Dwarves


They are short humanoid creatures usually 4 feet tall and weigh the same as a human. They are experts when it comes to combat, but rarely use magic. They have created dwarven technology, love to mine, and are addicted to gems, minerals, and precious metals.


Lizardfolk


Lizardfolk, weighing 200 to 250 pounds, are usually 6 to 7 feet tall and have either green, gray, or brown scales. It's tail is 3 to 4 feet long and used for balance. They usualy get allong well with other races, compared to humans. However, they are more primitive and are more savage.


Elves


Elves are usually 5 feet tall and weigh just over 100 pounds. Their diet consists of fruits and vegetables, but sometimes they hunt. They are cautious warriors, using ambushes, snipers, and camouflage at their advantage. If they are spotted they will retreat and attack the enemy again.


Fighter


Melee type characters that usually use a sword, mace, or axe to inflict damage. They can wield 2 swords, a sword and a shield, or a two handed sword.
