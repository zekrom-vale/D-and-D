We play a lot of games

Dungeons and dragons is a medieval game with kings, queens, knights, swords, bows, magic, and of course dungeons and dragons! The magic in this game can be helpful, hurtful, or just outright stupid. The most recent version is 5, developed by Wizards of the Cost.
Star Wars Star Wars is a futuristic style game, with blasters, droids, AI, and space travel. The two main factions are the Imperials and the Rebels. The Imperials have Storm Troopers, Imperial Pilots, Shock Troopers, the Sith lords, and enough money to buy bounty hunters. While the Rebels have Rebel Troops, Wookie's, Jedi, and people like Han Solo and Leia.
