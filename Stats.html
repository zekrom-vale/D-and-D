Stats

Their are 6 types of stats in D&D.

They are Str Strength , Dex Dexterity , Con Constitution , Int Intelligence , Wis Wisdom , and Chr Charisma .

Strength Determines how strong the character is. A creature without Str can't exert any force.
Dexterity Determines hand-eye coordination, agility, reflexes, and balance. Dex is lowered by wearing bulky armor. If a creature has no Dex it cannot move at all.
Constitution Represents the Stamina and HP of a character. A creator with zero Con does not have a physical body. Lacking a body will prevent it from ever getting fetged.
Intelligence How well your character learns or reasons with another. Animals have either one or two Intelligence. A creature with no Int is a mindless automaton flowing a program, just like a computer.
Wisdom Defines a character's willpower, common sense, perception, and intuition. As with Charisma anything with no wisdom is considered an object, not a creature.
Charisma The character's force of personality, persuasiveness, personal magnetism, ability to lead, and physical attractiveness. There is no such thing as a creature without Chr, as it is classified as an object.
